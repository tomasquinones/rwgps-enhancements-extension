# Privacy Policy — RideWithGPS Enhancements

**Last updated:** April 12, 2026

## Data Collection

RideWithGPS Enhancements does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Storage

The extension stores your feature preferences (which features are enabled, color settings, and UI state) locally in your browser using `browser.storage.local`. This data never leaves your device.

## Network Requests

The extension makes requests only to `ridewithgps.com` to fetch trip and route data that is already visible to you on the page. No data is sent to any third-party servers.

## Permissions

- **storage**: Used to save your feature toggle preferences locally.
- **host_permissions (ridewithgps.com)**: Required to inject visual enhancements on RideWithGPS pages and fetch trip/route track data from the RideWithGPS API.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the source repository:
https://github.com/tomasquinones/rwgps-enhancements-extension
